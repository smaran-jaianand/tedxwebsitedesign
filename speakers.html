Dr. Vidya Yeravdekar

Pro Chancellor | Symbiosis International ( Deemed University )

Dr. Vidya Yeravdekar is the Pro Chancellor of Symbiosis International (Deemed University), a premier multi-disciplinary university with over 45,000 students from across India and 85 countries. She holds postgraduate degrees in Medicine and Law, along with a Ph.D. in the Internationalisation of Higher Education in India. A key contributor to India's National Education Policy 2020, Dr. Yeravdekar has served two terms on the University Grants Commission (UGC) and held roles in national bodies like CABE, ICCR, SEPC, and IBEF. As Chairperson of FICCI's Higher Education Committee, she continues to shape education policy and promote 'Brand India' globally.She also serves on the boards of several academic, industry, and nonprofit organisations, and remains committed to driving innovation and global engagement at Symbiosis.

Harsha Durugadda

Sculptor

Harsha Durugadda is an Indian sculptor who received The Arts Family Emerging Artist Award in 2023 for South Asia and won the Rio Tinto Sculpture Award 2017 at Sculpture by the Sea, Australia. In 2021 he was part of a Sotheby’s New York contemporary auction in partnership with Burning Man. In 2014, the Courtauld Institute of Art invited him to present on Ancient Buddhist Sculpture at the British Museum, London. He received the Andrew Stretton Memorial invitation in 2016 Sydney. His sculptures have been exhibited internationally at Nord Art in Germany, Emergent Art Space in the United States, London, and Sculpture by the Sea in Australia. Durugadda’s artworks are part of major public and private collections including Busselton Jetty (Australia), Bangalore Airport and RMZ Foundation.
