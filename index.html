WHY TEDx ?

Imagine  walking on your terrace at night—mosquitoes buzzing, shadows shifting—while your mind wrestles with questions about life's boundaries. If constraints define limits, then TED redefines the essence of being limitless. TED—Technology, Entertainment, and Design—is a global platform where creativity flows freely and every idea, no matter how small, finds its voice. Now meet TEDx: the independent sibling, carrying the same spirit of innovation. The "x" stands for independently organized, but the mission is shared—ideas worth spreading. TEDx welcomes speakers of every tone, type, and tongue—from high-level business insights to heartfelt first-time flight stories that whisper truths about life. Rooted in local communities but driven by universal purpose, TEDx is powered by ideas as its soul, speakers as its passion, and people as its vision. It invites anyone with a meaningful thought to step forward, speak out, and unleash the power of experience.

WE THE SIUH . .

In a world woven with wildly diverse threads, the ideals beyond the surface are beautifully intertwined. Imagination is where the “beyond” begins—and TEDxSIUH invites it to take center stage. Here, our speakers uncap the infinite potential of the mind, diving deep into thought, passion, and experience. Life’s most disparate journeys often reshape who we are—each twist and turn leaving behind a truth: the world is unpredictable, and nothing is guaranteed. To grow, we must take risks. Play it safe, and the rewards remain ordinary. But when we dare to defy limits—when we challenge stereotypes and break barriers—we spark change. Not just in ourselves, but in everyone watching. True inspiration doesn’t come from outside. It begins within, and when it’s unleashed, it elevates everyone it touches.
