WHY TEDx ?

Imagine a space where boundaries dissolve. Imagine a space where creativity flows freely and every idea is given the chance to grow. That is the spirit of TED, a global platform built on Technology, Entertainment, and Design, where knowledge meets imagination and one idea can spark change across the world. TEDx brings this same vision to local communities. The “x” signifies independently organized, yet the mission remains unchanged: ideas worth spreading. Each TEDx event creates a stage for voices of every kind. Leaders share insights that influence industries. Innovators present solutions that challenge convention. Everyday individuals tell stories that reveal simple yet powerful truths about life. What makes TEDx unique is its connection to people. It is powered by ideas as its soul, by speakers as its passion, and by communities as its vision. At its heart, TEDx is an open invitation for anyone with a meaningful thought to step forward, speak with conviction, and unleash the transformative power of their experience.

WE THE SIUH . .

live in a world woven with diverse threads, where values beneath the surface are bound together in ways both subtle and powerful. Imagination is where the journey beyond begins, and we bring that imagination to the forefront through TEDxSIUH. Here, our speakers unlock the limitless potential of the mind, drawing on thought, passion, and experience. The many paths of life shape who we are, each turn reminding us that the world is uncertain and nothing can ever be taken for granted. Growth demands courage, because playing it safe leads only to ordinary outcomes. When we choose to go beyond limits, when we confront stereotypes and break barriers, we ignite transformation. That spark spreads outward, touching not only ourselves but everyone who listens and connects. Inspiration is not something we wait for, it begins within us, and once released, it carries the power to elevate all those it encounters.
